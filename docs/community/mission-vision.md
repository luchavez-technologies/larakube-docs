# 🎯 Mission & Vision

LaraKube is more than just a CLI tool; it is a commitment to elevating the Laravel development experience through the power of industrial-strength container orchestration.

## 🔭 The Vision
We envision a world where every Laravel developer can harness the full power of Kubernetes without needing a degree in cluster administration. By bringing "Production-First" architecture to the local environment, we eliminate the friction between development and deployment, allowing creators to focus on their vision.

## 🚀 The Mission
1.  **Democratize Orchestration**: Provide a zero-config entry point into the Kubernetes ecosystem.
2.  **Ensure Stability**: Automate complex infrastructure tasks like volume management and configuration syncing.
3.  **Encourage Mastery**: Build expressive tools that empower developers to architect their own custom cluster "masterpieces."
